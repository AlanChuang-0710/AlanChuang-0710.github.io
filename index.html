I grew up in Taiwan, which bears a variety of natural landscapes.The splendor of the steep mountain and the mystery of the forest were so attractive that I have developed an interest in trekking. Notable projects include Introduction to New Dosage, New Materials Research, and Intellectual Property Content Management System Construction.

I am seeking a Front-end Engineering position. With my experience and drive to keep learning and improving, I am open to any challenges that I may face.
